Privacy Policy for BarberQueue

Last updated: November 21, 2025

BarberQueue ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use BarberQueue, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name (from Google Sign-In)
  • User Profile: Role (barber or client), account creation date
  • Shop Information (for barbers): Shop name, address, location coordinates (latitude/longitude), shop photos
  • Worker Information: Worker names, phone numbers, email addresses, active status

1.2 Queue and Booking Data

  • Client names and phone numbers
  • Queue position and status
  • Booking timestamps
  • Walk-in vs. pre-booked status

1.3 Location Data

We collect and process location data to:

  • Display shop locations on maps
  • Help clients find nearby barbershops
  • Enable location-based search functionality

1.4 Device Information

  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to send queue updates and notifications
  • Device identifiers: Used for authentication and service delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide queue management, booking, and notification services
  • Authentication: To verify your identity using Google Sign-In
  • Communication: To send push notifications about queue status, your turn, and shop updates
  • Shop Management: To enable barbers to manage their shops, workers, and client queues
  • Location Services: To display shop locations and enable location-based search
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: All user data, shop information, and queue data is stored in Google's Firebase Cloud Firestore with industry-standard encryption
  • Firebase Authentication: User authentication is handled securely through Firebase Auth
  • Google Cloud Platform: Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

BarberQueue integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and push notifications
  • Google Maps: For displaying shop locations and maps

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers (Google/Firebase) who assist in operating our app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Between Users: Shop information and queue status are visible to clients; client names and positions are visible to shop owners and workers

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through the app settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Location Services: You can disable location permissions in your device settings
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings

7. Data Deletion Request

If you wish to request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to bmazen85@gmail.com
  • Include the email address associated with your BarberQueue account
  • Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - BarberQueue"
  • In the email body, clearly state that you wish to delete your account and all associated data

Upon receiving your request, we will:

  • Verify your identity to ensure the security of your data
  • Process your deletion request within 30 days
  • Send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted
  • Permanently delete all your personal information, including account details, shop information (if applicable), queue history, and any other data associated with your account

Please note: Once your data is deleted, this action cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use BarberQueue again in the future.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

BarberQueue is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
